Welcome to Echelon Dev Society

The official tech club of Chameli Devi Group of Institutions (CDGI). We are a community of innovators and tech enthusiasts dedicated to inspiring, educating, and empowering students to thrive in the ever-evolving world of technology.

  • Hackathons: Solve real-world challenges with creativity and innovation.
  • Expert Talks: Gain insights from industry leaders.
  • Mentorship Programs: Get guidance from experienced professionals to boost your skills.
  • Workshops and Projects: Hands-on learning and collaboration on impactful projects.
